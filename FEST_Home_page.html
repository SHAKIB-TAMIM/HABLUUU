Hablu's Heartbreak

A lazy genius. A campus crush. A CGPA crisis.

Hablu, an overly relaxed student at PUST, spends his days lying in bed, scrolling Facebook, and dreaming big. One afternoon, inspired by a motivational reel, he awkwardly asked his crush Sokhina:

"Sokhina, shuno. What if we sit with the Imam and say the capital of Afghanistan three times?"

Sokhina, unimpressed, gave him a reality check: fix your CGPA, learn real skills, and maybe start dressing better.

Thus begins Hablu's redemption journey...

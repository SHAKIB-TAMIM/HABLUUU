Dress Better: Fashion Advice

Sokhina often teases Hablu about his collection of free T-shirts from various hackathons. It's time to update his wardrobe! Hablu wants fashion advice that fits his body type, suits his student budget, and helps him look sharp both socially and professionally.

A good wardrobe makes a lasting first impression. Let's explore some options for both formal and casual styles!

Formal Look

Perfect for interviews, meetings, and professional events. A well-fitted suit from brands like Arong or Top Ten will elevate your appearance.

Casual Look

Great for daily wear and social outings. Pair a well-fitted tee and chinos from Top Ten or Arong for a stylish and comfortable look.
